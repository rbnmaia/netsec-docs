HackTheBox (HTB) and TryHackMe (THM)

HTB is a penetration testing platform with many machines that feel like they belong in the OSCP labs. All you have to do is pass the registration challenge and only then, you will have your VPN access provided. I suggest doing a few as it is free and an excellent way to prepare for the exam without downloading a vulnerable VM.&#x20;
